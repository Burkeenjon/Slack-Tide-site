Slack Tide – 7-Part Story Structure with Chapter Breakdown

1. Hook (Chapters 1–2)

Sara infiltrates a remote estate outside Bogotá. The Solace gala unfolds in Vienna. The duality is stark.

Chapter 1: Intercut – Sara on mission, Adrien at gala.

Chapter 2: Sara refuses the kill order and vanishes, setting the tone for her break from Solace.

2. Inciting Incident (Chapter 3)

Sara severs contact, disables her tracking, and disappears into the open ocean. She becomes a ghost.

Chapter 3: Descent into the sea. First signs of psychological stress. Sara sets a course for the Pacific.

3. First Plot Point (Chapters 4–5)

Sara joins a drifting cruiser fleet—'the cursers.' The anonymity offers peace but doesn’t erase her past.

Chapter 4: Arrival in the cruiser anchorage. Introduction of new faces.

Chapter 5: Flashbacks intercut with her cautious bonding in the community.

4. Midpoint / Reversal (Chapters 6–9)

A coded message or encounter reveals Solace’s ops are still tracking her. Worse—she learns sparing the child didn’t stop the operation.

Chapter 6: Signal detected.

Chapter 7: A boat from the past docks nearby. Tension.

Chapter 8: She confronts the cruiser who brought the signal.

Chapter 9: Sara realizes the shadow branch is deeper than Adrien’s reach.

5. Second Plot Point (Chapters 10–12)

Her cover is blown. A friend is hurt. Sara goes on the defensive.

Chapter 10: Local betrayal. Traced via supplies or systems.

Chapter 11: Fight or flight. Tactical retreat into the outer atolls.

Chapter 12: She recovers data proving shadow ops activities.

6. Climax (Chapters 13–15)

Sara executes a silent op. No blood. No bodies. Only exposure.

Chapter 13: Infiltration prep using resources from her cruising contacts.

Chapter 14: Data release – leak to someone outside the system (journalist, watchdog, or Solace board).

Chapter 15: Aftermath – Solace responds publicly, Adrien appears rattled, unaware of full extent.

7. Resolution (Chapters 16–17)

Sara sails solo into open water. She’s free—but alone.

Chapter 16: Goodbyes, quietly handled. She gifts tools, not explanations.

Chapter 17: Onboard Nymeria, she reflects, plans no future—but leaves no traces either.

Epilogue – Echo Below

A ghost signal from a dead drop. A dive to recover a cache: flash drive, journal, and WWII coordinates.

A forgotten sub base in an uncharted lagoon.

The next journey waits beneath the waves.
